Our Courses

Accredited qualifications that open real career doors.

These courses are ideal for candidates who don't have a Grade 12 qualification but want to enter a career path in health, development and social care. Learners with a Grade 12 pass have an added advantage.

Training opens doors to careers as a pharmacy assistant, medical secretary, care giver or community health worker — and with further study can lead toward nursing. Courses lead to national certificates awarded by the Health and Welfare Sector Education and Training Authority (HWSETA) and the Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO).
