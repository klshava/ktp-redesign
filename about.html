Our Story

A trusted name in health & social care training.

Since 2015

From a small cohort to over 1,000 graduates.

Kunokhanya Trading and Projects (Pty) Ltd was founded and registered with CIPRO (2015/250399/07) in 2015. In 2016 the academy received Further Education and Training College accreditation from HWSETA and registered with the Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO).

Training began in 2017 with our first intake. Since then, Kunokhanya has grown to welcome around 200 new learners a year, supported by a team of more than eight staff members — and we've now graduated over 1,000 learners into careers in health and social care.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the centre of excellence for the development and successful delivery of outcomes-based education and training in the Health Sciences and Social Services industry in South Africa.

🤝 Our Mission

We are committed to regulatory compliance, creating engaging educational experiences, building strong stakeholder partnerships, and being a trusted educational provider — not just a profit-focused enterprise.

Our People

Led by experienced educators and health professionals.

Our team is led by a Managing Director and Principal, supported by an administrative team and five dedicated facilitators — bringing international, national and local experience in education and the health sciences.

🌍 Our Networks

We partner with care facilities, community organisations and corporate clients — including Wits Health Hubb and Transcend Corporate Advisors — to give learners real-world exposure and placement opportunities.

📚 Our Approach

Every course blends theory with hands-on practice, so learners graduate confident, work-ready, and equipped for real careers in the health and social care sectors.
